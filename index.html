We're running three campaigns, each featuring a differing playstyle and set of objectives with a DM specializing in the worldbuilding for that campaign. This way, you can choose what's best and most fun for you!

Flexible Scheduling and Reliable Recaps

Our campaign groups meet once per month at 3:00pm on the Saturday of that month which receives the most votes by group members. However, if you can't make a session, each DM has been tasked with reaching out to you to provide a full recap of the key events you may have missed.
